Our Story

We'll be celebrating our ninth anniversary as a couple in September.
See how our story started!

2007
August 2007

The Day we Met

We met on the second day of college at Temple University during a freshman scavenger hunt run by the Honors program. Brian dunked his head in a bucket of ice water and ate three bananas in 45 seconds to help the team win. Julie was obviously impressed. The rest is history.
September 2007

Our First Dates

We got to know each other better during that first month of college. We went on a few memorable dates to a Phillies game (after which Brian ended up in the ER with an allergic reaction!), a dinner at Spaghetti Warehouse and a trip to the Camden Aquarium.
2011
May 2011

Graduation Day

After four years of all-nighters at the Tech Center, dinners at the SAC, parties in Kardon, basketball games on Broad, and late nights at Lucy's, we graduated from Temple surrounded by our best friends.
2013
May 2013

Moving Day

After two years of long distance and 100 miles between them, Brian joined Julie in New York City. When we're not at home in Harlem (or stuck on the subway), you can find us brunching with friends, enjoying Broadway shows or trying local brews at the bar.
2015
August 2015

Our First Vacation

Last summer, we enjoyed our first vacation together in Disney World! It was a magical four days for both of us as we nerded out in Harry Potter World, drank around the world in Epcot and, of course, enjoyed the fireworks in Magic Kingdom. Julie got the feeling that something even more magical might be coming soon...
September 2015

Our Engagement

Just a few weeks later, as we celebrated our eighth anniversary with dinner at Dos Caminos and a sunset stroll, Brian popped the question! The proposal took place on The High Line, which is (and now always will be!) our favorite park in the city.

ASHLEY MEARSBridesmaid

SHANNON QUILESBridesmaid

GARY CARNEVALEGroomsman
